# How to Set Up Hooper's Path

A 60-second walkthrough for new players.

## Step 1: Open the link on your iPhone (use Safari)

Tap or type: **https://hooperspath.com**

⚠️ Important: open it in Safari, not Chrome or in-app browsers (like
Instagram or TikTok). Sign in with Apple needs Safari to work right.

## Step 2: Sign in with Apple

1. Tap "Sign in with Apple"
2. Use Face ID / Touch ID / passcode to confirm
3. You can choose to share your name and email, or hide them — either works.
4. You may see a "Continue to the App" page briefly. That's normal.

## Step 3: Add it to your home screen

Once you're signed in, the app works in Safari, but it works **way better**
when added to your home screen — full-screen, no browser bars.

1. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow up — bottom of Safari)
2. Scroll down in the share menu
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right

A "Hooper's Path" icon appears on your home screen. Open from there.

## Step 4: Set up your profile

- Enter your first name
- Enter your age (13+ only)
- Pick your team color
- Tap **Start →**

## Step 5: Try the daily workout

- The Today tab shows your workout for the day
- First, do the morning **check-in** (sleep, soreness, energy)
- Pick where you're training (full court, half court, hoop only, no hoop, etc.)
- Tap **Start Today's Work**

That's it. Train. The app does the rest.

## Tips

- **It works offline.** Once it loads, you can use it on the bus, in the gym
  with no signal, anywhere.
- **Streak counter** is the orange number at the top right. Don't break it.
- **Tap any drill** in the workout to see how to do it (with diagrams).
- **Log games** from Today tab → Log Game button.
- **Settings → Send Feedback** if anything's broken or missing.

## Frequently Asked

**Q: Is this free?**
Yes. Free now and forever for early testers like you.

**Q: Why Sign in with Apple?**
Lets your stats sync across devices. Your data is private — we don't see
your email unless you choose to share it.

**Q: What if I don't have an iPhone?**
The app currently works best on iPhone. Android support is coming.

**Q: How long should the workout take?**
30-45 minutes most days. Some days are recovery (15-20 min). Game day prep
is shorter.

**Q: I don't have a hoop right now.**
No problem — pick "No Hoop" or "Home Gym" environment. The app swaps out
shooting drills for ball-handling and strength work.

**Q: Where's my data stored?**
Locally on your phone, plus synced to Firebase under your Apple ID.
Only you can see it.

**Q: How do I cancel?**
There's nothing to cancel — it's free and you don't enter any payment info.
If you want your data deleted, Settings → Erase My Data.
